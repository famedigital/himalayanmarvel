[The Healing Power of Bhutan]

Heading: Menchu — The Himalayan Mineral Ritual
No relaxation vacation is complete without the Menchu. This traditional Bhutanese hot stone bath utilizes river stones heated by wood fires until they glow red. When dropped into glacial water infused with medicinal Khempa (Artemisia) leaves, they release a potent blend of minerals that soothe joint pain and melt away corporate stress. We arrange these rituals in private, scenic settings where the steam rises against a backdrop of snow-capped mountains.
