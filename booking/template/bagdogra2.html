02
Phuentsholing to Thimphu • Into the Kingdom
Drive: ~5 hours
Altitude: 2,320m
Route: Scenic Mountain Road

Morning immigration processing at the Bhutanese border office – your first official entry into the Kingdom! On the way to Thimphu, visit Sangye Migyur Ling Lhakhang in Phuntsholing – a remarkable temple inspired by Bhutanese Karma Kagyu master Lama Kelzang. This sacred site is a replica of an 11th century structure erected by the Tibetan medieval poet-yogi Milarepa. Continue the beautiful ascent to Thimphu, winding through terraced fields and mountain villages. Arrive in Thimphu by afternoon and check into your hotel. Evening visit to Tashichodzong to witness the illuminated fortress at sunset – a magical sight for the whole family.

"As you climb into the clouds, the modern world fades away, and the timeless magic of Bhutan embraces you."

04
Thimphu to Punakha • Mountain Pass
Drive: ~3 hours
High Point: Dochula La 3,150m
Night: Punakha

Morning drive over Dochula Pass – a mountain saddle adorned with 108 chortens (stupas). On clear days, you'll see panoramic views of the entire eastern Himalayan range. Descend into the warmer Punakha Valley. Visit the magnificent Punakha Dzong, arguably Bhutan's most beautiful fortress, situated at the confluence of two rivers. Walk across the dramatic suspension bridge for thrilling views and great family photos. Evening at leisure in this sub-tropical paradise.

"108 stupas watch over the mountain pass, guarding the ancient route to the valleys below."

06
Tiger's Nest Trek • The Sacred Journey
Hike: 4-5 hours
Climb: ~900m
Night: Paro

The big day has arrived! Today you'll trek to Bhutan's most iconic landmark – Taktsang Palphug Monastery (Tiger's Nest), perched dramatically on a cliff 900 meters above the valley floor. Legend says Guru Rinpoche flew here on the back of a tigress. The hike takes about 4-5 hours round trip through beautiful pine forests. Your experienced guide will set a comfortable family pace with plenty of rest stops along the way. At the top, visit the main temple, photograph breathtaking views, and enjoy lunch at the cafeteria. Your 9-year-old will feel incredibly proud of this achievement! Evening return to hotel with optional hot stone bath experience (not included in package – available on request).

"To reach the Tiger's Nest is to touch the sacred – a journey that transforms not just the body, but the soul."

Terms & Conditions
Booking & Payment

A 30% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm your booking. The remaining balance must be paid 30 days before your arrival date. We accept wire transfers, UPI, and all major credit cards. For Indian guests, payments can be made in INR equivalent.

Cancellation Policy

Full refund (minus deposit) for cancellations made 60+ days before departure. 50% refund for cancellations 30-60 days before. No refund within 30 days of departure unless covered by travel insurance.

Tiger's Nest Trek

The Tiger's Nest trek is suitable for families with children aged 7 and above. Our experienced guides will ensure a safe, enjoyable journey at a comfortable pace. Horses can be arranged for uphill climb if needed (additional cost applies).

Travel Documents

Valid passports (minimum 6 months validity) are required for all travelers, including children. Indian nationals can travel with Voter ID or Passport for entry into Bhutan via land.

Health & Fitness

A reasonable level of fitness is required for the Tiger's Nest trek. We recommend consulting your doctor before undertaking high-altitude trekking. Inform us of any medical conditions in advance.

Silverpine Bhutan acts as an agent for hotels, transport, and service providers. We shall not be liable for any injury, damage, loss, delay, or irregularity caused by defects in services or acts of God.
